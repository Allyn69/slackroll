Welcome to the *slackroll wiki*! _slackroll_ is a package or upgrade manager for Slackware Linux.

* Every new @slackroll@ user should follow the *[[tutorial]]* first. It contains information about how it works and instructions on the initial program set up process.
* Advanced users looking for specific information about one of its operations may find the *[[operations guide]]* useful.
* In case of trouble, or to find out some tips and tricks, read the *[[FAQ]]*.
